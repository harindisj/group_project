Get your personality to shine in your CV

Personality is what separates you from an invisible person on paper to someone who can bring more than just qualifications to a company. You are unique and your CV will be much more engaging if your personality shines throughout.

Make sure your CV is no more than two pages long, and includes just the bare minimum relavant information - the key is no waffle! The average time spent reading a CV is a mere 30 seconds. Everything an employer reads during those precious 30 seconds must be relevant and engaging.

Sometimes it's difficult to show off your strengths...
Writing about yourself can sometimes be difficult. Asking a trusted friend, or better, colleague is also a good way to find how others view your strengths.
This will allow you to build a portfolio of strengths and acheivments which you can apply to all applications.

Show you're more than just an employee!
It is a good idea to have a small section on your personal interests – this can provide the employer with a flavour of your personality.

Try to avoid using clichés.
Nearly every application will state they have excellent ‘communication’ or ‘organisational’ skills. If your application reads the same as everyone elses then you won’t stand out.
