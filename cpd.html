Continuing Professional Development

Continuing professional development is key in all workplaces. Personal growth shouldn't stop even after working somewhere for 10 years! You should always be looking for ways to improve and develop.
This is also really important if you're interested in progressing to managerial roles later in your career.
Scroll down for some fun ideas on how to be the best at your job!

Previous Next


Organisation
Keep a learning log to note down all the skills you learn on a weekly basis. Ask your line manager or colleagues for feedback or new ideas for important skills to improve on.

Have you thought about online courses?
If you choose to do an online course alongside your job, plan your time carefully so that you maintain a healthy work-life balance.

Reflection
It's so important to reflect. Without no reflection, there is no growth. You can use your log book, as we said before, to look back on your accomplishments and focus those skills learnt to the future.

Get coding!
More than ever, companies are now requiring some evidence of programming skills from their potential candidates. CodeFirst: Girls are a an organisation that work with men and women alike to get more women into tech. They offer both paid and free courses - go check them out by clicking here!
